# 🌱 GreenMind AI

> AI-Powered Carbon Footprint Awareness & Sustainability Platform

GreenMind AI helps individuals understand, track, and reduce their carbon footprint through personalized AI insights, sustainability coaching, predictive simulations, and gamified eco-challenges.

---

## Problem Statement

Climate change is one of the biggest global challenges today, yet most people struggle to understand how their daily activities impact the environment.

Individuals often lack:

* Awareness of their carbon footprint
* Personalized sustainability guidance
* Tools to measure environmental impact
* Motivation to adopt eco-friendly habits

GreenMind AI bridges this gap by combining carbon footprint tracking, artificial intelligence, analytics, and gamification into a single platform that empowers users to make sustainable lifestyle choices.

---

## Solution Overview

GreenMind AI is an AI-powered sustainability platform that enables users to:

* Calculate their carbon footprint
* Receive AI-generated sustainability recommendations
* Interact with an eco-focused AI assistant
* Compare current vs eco-friendly lifestyles
* Simulate sustainability scenarios
* Track environmental progress
* Earn badges and rewards
* Monitor sustainability performance through analytics

The platform transforms sustainability from a complex concept into actionable daily decisions.
